Bike Shop App

  • A dynamic e-commerce system with roles for Admins and Clients, enabling secure registration, login, and logout, while admins manage customers, products, and orders, and clients manage carts, submit orders, and view order history.
  • Robust order management includes user-specific order tracking, real-time total price calculation, and secure SQL-based storage with commit and rollback mechanisms to ensure data integrity.
  • Scalable and modular design allows for easy expansion, supporting features like payment processing, inventory management, and analytics, while prioritizing maintainability and future growth.
